🔍 Queries de referência rápida  Ver guia completo →

  • 📈 PromQL — CPU: sum(rate(container_cpu_usage_seconds_total{name!=""}[1m])) by (name) * 100
  • 💾 PromQL — RAM: container_memory_usage_bytes{name!=""}
  • 📝 LogQL — todos os logs: {job="docker"}
  • 💥 LogQL — só erros: {job="docker"} |= "ERROR"
  • 🔎 LogQL — logs do simulator: {tag="simulator"}
  • 🌐 LogQL — logs do nginx: {tag="nginx-app"} |= "404"
